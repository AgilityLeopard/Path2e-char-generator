Tower Builder

Objective

    In this game you will take turns to move builders and build portions of castle towers. If you are able to move your builder to the top of a completed three-storey tower, you win the game! You can also win the game if all your opponent's builders become trapped between towers and cannot make any legal moves.

Gameplay

On your turn you will perform three actions.

  1. Select one of your builders to move
  2. Move the builder to an adjacent square. Builders can move forwards, backwards, sideways, and diagonally. Builders may move one level up or any number of levels down.
  3. Build a portion of a castle tower. Building on the ground will build a tower base. Building on a base will build a midsection. Building on a midsection completes the tower. If you build ontop of a completed tower, it becomes roofed. Roofed towers cannot be climbed on top of.

Note: to select builders, move, or build, click on the ground tile that they are above.
