Join Us

We are looking for both junior professionals who are new to the industry, and senior professionals who are experienced in program evaluation, labour market research, economic development, science and technology, trade development and investment attraction, and public policy research.

We offer the potential for a rewarding career in a challenging, fast paced and dynamic work team-based environment. We are committed to creating a positive and flexible work environment which provides our staff with the opportunities to perform meaningful and interesting assignments for a wide range of clients in the public, private and non-profit sectors.
