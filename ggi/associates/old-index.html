What is the GGI Platform?

The GGI Platform brings together our growing network of Associates with leading edge management, communication, research and analytical tools, and strong marketing to create opportunities for our Associates and offer an ever-increasing range of high quality, leading-edge services for our clients.

At the foundation of GGI Platform is a searchable database that contains data on all of our associates. We draw from this database when developing effective project teams for our existing or proposed projects. We can also use this database to link our associates to clients who approach us as well as to other associates who are constructing teams for their projects.

To support our Associates, we will be making significant investments in leading edge management, communication, research and analytical tools and resources as well as marketing programs and tools. Over the next year, we will also be developing a series of new products and services that will incorporate services delivered by our associates and ourselves.

Who is GGI?

Since its establishment in 1981, Goss Gilroy Inc. (GGI) has grown into one of the leading independent management consulting firms in Canada. Through our offices across Canada, our team of 40 professionals, and our growing network of over 100 associates, we deliver a wide range of consulting services to government, the non-profit sector and industry in each province and territory as well as internationally.

Why Have We Developed the GGI Platform?

We established the GGI Platform as a means to respond to the significant changes occurring in the consulting industry. We anticipate that the industry will change more in the next five to ten years than it has in the past 40 years. For example:

  • Clients increasingly want specialists, rather than generalists. However, they still prefer seamless experiences (not having to deal directly with four different consulting firms), which increases the importance of being able to develop teams involving people with a wide range of technical and subject matter skills from inside and outside of the firm.
  • With the emergence of the Gig economy, more and more consultants are working independently or in small groups. Attracting and maintaining staff is a major challenge for firms such as ours.
  • Technological change is accelerating and will impact how we market our services, compile our project teams, deliver our services, and communicate.
  • The time and costs associated with keeping up with the latest technology (e.g. project management, survey, and text, social media, big data, and predictive analytics software, and infographics and visualization tools) are soaring.

The GGI Platform is designed to assist GGI in responding to that change by facilitating the development of project teams, increasing our access to the specialists, and giving us the critical mass needed to invest in leading edge management, communication and research tools and resources.

How Would We Work Together?

There are three ways that we commonly work with our Associates:

  • We may call on you to be a member of one of our project teams when your skills and experience best meet the requirements of a project we have landed or a proposed project on which we are bidding. We may also link you directly to clients who approach us as well as to other associates who are constructing teams for their projects.
  • We can work with on project opportunities that you identify. We can provide assistance to you in:
  • Building a winning project team, by referring you to other associates as well as to members of our staff;
  • Preparing the proposal. We have a dedicated group of proposal writers that can assist. Our brand and history may also improve your chances of being the winning bid; and
  • Implementing the project. We can provide you with access to our research team and leading-edge project management and analytical tools. We are making significant investments into sophisticated software tools (project management, collaboration, survey, and text, social media, big data, and predictive analytics software, and infographics and visualization tools) and have access to leading-edge practitioners within both our research group and our network of associates. We also have senior members on staff who can provide project management leadership or support, if and when needed.
  • We can work with you on joint marketing efforts. We have a team of experts who are involved in marketing services to a wide range of prospective clients.

What Does It Cost?

There is no cost to you of becoming a GGI Associate. If we engage you to work on one of our projects, we would negotiate the fees with you and typically apply a small markup on those fees when invoicing the client. If you engage our services to assist you in completing a project, we would first negotiate a price for those services with you.

Would I Be Committed to Work With GGI?

You make no commitment to work with us or to be a member of any of our project teams (we first check with you before including you in a proposal or making client referrals). There are no restrictions whatsoever on your ability to pursue other work or work with other firms.

What are the Requirements to Become an Associate?

Decisions are made on a case-by-case basis. In recruiting associates, we are generally looking for people who have at least two years experience in delivering management consulting or program evaluation services or have extensive experience in a senior position within government, a non-profit organization or industry. We are also looking for people with highly specialized technical skills, such as skills related to new technology, software or methodologies.
