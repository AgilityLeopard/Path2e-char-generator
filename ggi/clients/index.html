Prospective Clients

We established the GGI Platform to respond to the significant changes occurring in the consulting industry. The industry will change more in the next five to ten years than it has in the past 40 years. We know that:

  • Clients increasingly want specialists, rather than generalists. However, you still prefer seamless experiences (not having to deal directly with four different consulting firms), which means that we need to be more adept at constructing teams involving people from inside and outside our firm.
  • With the emergence of the Gig economy, more and more consultants are working independently or in small groups. This adds to the challenge we face in attracting and maintaining staff. It can also make it difficult for clients to find consultants with the specific skills they are looking for.
  • Technological change is accelerating and will impact how we market our services, compile our project teams, deliver our services, and communicate. The time and costs associated with keeping up with the latest technology (e.g. project management, survey, and text, social media, big data, and predictive analytics software, and infographics and visualization tools) are soaring.

The GGI Platform is designed to benefit clients by providing you with:

  • Greater access to consultants with highly specialized skills
  • Access to new products and services. We will be launching a series of new, next generation products and services over the next 12 to 18 months
  • Access to leading-edge management, collaboration, communication, research and analytics tools and resources
  • More seamless experiences. We can offer a wider range of services to you, tailored to meet your specific needs.
  • Shorter timeliness associated with development and implementation of the projects.

If you have any questions or have a service in mind on which you would like a quote, please contact us. If it’s not a management consulting service we normally provide, it’s almost certain that one of our Associates does.

If you’d like to keep up-to-date with our progress in launching the GGI Platform as well as the introduction of new products and services, please subscribe to our newsletter.
