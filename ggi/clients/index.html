Clients tell us that their world is changing. Quickly.
This is how we're responding.

What You Told Us

Clients Prefer Specialists

Clients increasingly want specialists, rather than generalists. However, you still prefer seamless experiences (not having to deal directly with four different consulting firms), which means that we need to be more adept at constructing teams involving people from inside and outside our firm.

Finding Consultants with Specific Skills is Challenging

With the emergence of the gig economy, more and more consultants are working independently or in small groups. This adds to the challenge consulting firms face in attracting and maintaining staff. It can also make it difficult for clients to find consultants with the specific skills they are looking for.

Technological change is accelerating

This technological change will impact how we market our services, compile our project teams, deliver our services, and communicate. The time and costs associated with keeping up with the latest technology (e.g. project management, survey, and text, social media, big data, and predictive analytics software, and infographics and visualization tools) are soaring.

How We're Responding

Greater access to consultants with highly specialized skills

Access to new products and services, launching within the next 12 to 18 months

Shorter timelines associated with development and implementation of the projects.

More seamless experiences. We can offer a wider range of services to you, tailored to meet your specific needs.

Access to leading-edge management, collaboration, communication, research and analytics tools and resources

If you have any questions or have a service in mind on which you would like a quote, please contact us. If it’s not a management consulting service we normally provide, it’s almost certain that one of our Associates does.

What The GGI Platform Means For You

When we first explain the GGI Platform to our clients, we often hear, "Well, that sounds interesting, but what does it mean for us?"

Simply put, the GGI platform means that we can now provide you with a wider range of innovative high-quality services, delivered more quickly and cost effectively than ever before by a strong and diverse team of consultants tailored to your specific needs.

If you have a research need, it's likely that either we have extensive experience in that area or a member of our network of Associates does.
