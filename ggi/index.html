GGI Platform

Goss Gilroy Inc. (GGI) is excited to formally introduce the GGI Platform.

The management consulting industry is entering a period of unprecedented change. Clients increasingly want teams of specialists, rather than generalists. Consultants are under ever greater pressure to reduce timelines and costs. At the same time, new technologies have the potential to significantly improve how we market our services, create our project teams, deliver our services, and communicate with clients and associates but often come at a significant cost.

The GGI Platform is designed to help us in responding to these changes by:

  • Growing our network of Associates, which enables us to quickly create project teams that feature the range of technical and subject matter skills needed by our existing and prospective clients.
  • Providing our clients and project teams with access to leading edge management, communication, research and analytical tools.
  • Making significant investments in marketing and proposal development.

GGI Platform will enable us to offer an ever-increasing range of high quality, leading-edge services to our clients and create significant new business opportunities our Associates and ourselves.

GGI Platform is a new initiative for us, but one that is firmly rooted in the way we’ve always done business. Most of our services already involve team members from outside of the firm and, in fact, many of our projects are led by our Associates. What has changed is that we recognize this isn't just a preferred way of doing business, it is now an essential part of our business.
