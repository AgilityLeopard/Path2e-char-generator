Privacy Policy

AT GGI, YOUR PRIVACY IS VERY IMPORTANT TO US

We are committed to maintaining the security and privacy of the information we collect from the public, our clients and our associates. GGI is a member of the Marketing Research and Intelligence Association (MRIA), an organization that sets industry standards to which member companies must adhere and which also protect your privacy. Our privacy policies are based on MRIA values and compliant with Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). “Personal information” is defined in PIPEDA as information about an identifiable individual, although it does not include the name, business title, business address or telephone number of employees of organizations.

COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

The GGI Platform may collect personally-identifiable information on certain areas of the site that you provide voluntarily. This information is collected for the purposes of identifying opportunities for us to work together. For example, if you register to become an associate, you may provide information on your education, skills, experience, interests, and other background information and add it to your profile (“Profile”). You may edit your Profile by revisiting your profile by using the personalized link and can ask that we delete your Profile at any time.

ACCESS TO INFORMATION

Access to information collected through our web site is restricted to staff working on the GGI Platform and GGI employees who require the information for identifying potential projects on which we could work with you, developing project teams, preparing bids and developing an overview of our associate network. Our staff is trained to respect the rules of data integrity and confidentiality.

SHARING OF INFORMATION

The information you provide will not be shared with other parties, including other associate members of the GGI Platform, without your consent. We will not, under any circumstances, share, sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any individual information collected on our site to any third party, without your express permission. GGI will never use email addresses provided to us to send ‘spam’, or unsolicited commercial emails.

LEAVING THE WEBSITE

Our Privacy Policy will no longer apply once you leave this website (www.GGIplatform.ca). We encourage you to refer to the privacy policies posted on third party sites.

ANALYTICS

There are certain types of information about visitors that is kept by user-logs on the web server. This information includes the pages visited, the IP address of visitors, time spent at the site, etc. This information is used by GGI to improve the site and manage site traffic. No individual identifying information is collected.
