Our Values

Offering a Great Place to Work

GGI believes in a dynamic work environment that provides opportunities for staff fulfillment and development.

Committed to Service Excellence

GGI always keeps the clients’ needs at the centre of our work and delivers high quality products.

Doing Work of Social Significance

GGI is a collection of passionate individuals who contribute, through our work, to the public good in Canada and around the world.

Contributing to and Building our Community

GGI gives back to our community in many ways, including supporting our staff to volunteer with professional associations, openly sharing best practices with other professionals and embracing knowledge sharing through hybrid teams with our clients
