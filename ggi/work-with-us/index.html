Join Us — We're Hiring!

Full Time - Senior Level - Principal

Goss Gilroy Inc. (GGI) is seeking senior level candidates for the position of Principal. As a senior member of the GGI team, the Principal is responsible for building long-term client relationships; leading complex and strategic projects while applying proven project management techniques; providing mentoring and coaching to staff; and developing new and existing business markets. The Principal is expected to play a significant hands-on role in the delivery of innovative projects to a predominately government-based client group.

Join Us as an Associate

Our associates are experienced consultants, evaluators, senior managers, and others with specialized skills and expertise relevant to clients. By joining the GGI Platform, you will continue to enjoy the benefits and lifestyle that comes with working independently while gaining access to benefits that come with working with a large consulting firm.

Some associates look at the GGI Platform as simply an additional source of work. However, most are drawn by opportunities to pursue larger projects, access leading-edge tools and resources, get assistance in marketing, proposal development and project implementation, and become a member of the GGI community.

There is no cost to you of becoming a GGI Associate. If you work on one of our projects, we negotiate the fees with you and typically apply a markup when invoicing the client. Where we assist you in obtaining or completing a project, we would negotiate the terms for those services with you.

Goss Gilroy Inc. (GGI), a leading management consulting firm specializing in program evaluation and management consulting, is seeking senior level candidates for the position of Principal. As a senior member of the GGI team, the Principal is responsible for building long-term client relationships; leading complex and strategic projects while applying proven project management techniques; providing mentoring and coaching to staff; and developing new and existing business markets. The Principal is expected to play a significant hands on role in the delivery of innovative projects to a predominately government based client group.

Educational Requirement

Post secondary degree at the Master’s level.

Language Requirement

Ability to work in both English and French is preferred.

Work Experience Requirement
  • Minimum of ten years experience in a consulting role with an emphasis on project management.
  • Minimum of five years experience in a people leadership role with strong experience in coaching and staff development.
  • Knowledge of the machinery of government.
  • Superior written and verbal communication, as well as strong presentation skills.
  • Proven ability to work collaboratively and effectively with a variety of highly talented professionals in a team-based work environment.
  • Experience in program evaluation or organizational development at the government level is required.
Company Description:

GGI’s approach to consulting services has been shaped by over 29 years of assignments with the public sector at the federal, provincial and municipal levels and in the private and non-profit sectors in Canada and internationally.

With offices in Ottawa and St. John’s, GGI enjoys an excellent reputation as a leading firm providing consulting services, primarily to government. GGI prides itself on the level of senior level involvement in consulting projects and has been rewarded with a high level of repeat business and fully satisfied clients. With the support of our team of talented and dedicated consulting professionals we have successfully completed over 2800 consulting assignments.
